Welcome.

This website deals with topics surrounding child marriage in India. These topics can be distressing and also largely sickening to many not familiar with the stories. I ask all to keep an open mind and also an open heart when thinking about these real women and their painful struggles. This is not a reflection of the beautiful country of India nor its people.On the contrary it is a showcase of culture while acknowledging its flaws that have devastating consequences, often for young girls. Although it is saddening these issues continue to be prevalent, we must turn our focus to the ways we perpetuate unrealistic living standards in our minds and try to comprehend the depth of these people's lives. Enjoy this website and I hope these issues find an audience who understand that our past and present does not have to define our futures.
